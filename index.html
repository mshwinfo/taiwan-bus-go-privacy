隱私權政策 Privacy Policy

繁體中文

最後更新日期：2026 年 6 月 21 日

感謝您使用「台灣等公車」（以下稱「本 App」）。本 App 重視您的隱私，本政策說明我們如何處理您的資料。

一、我們蒐集與使用的資料

1. 位置資訊

  • 當您使用「附近站牌」功能時，本 App 會請求您的裝置位置（概略或精確位置），用於查詢您附近的公車站牌。
  • 位置僅在您主動使用該功能時取得，並透過我們的查詢代理伺服器轉送至交通部 TDX 以取得附近站牌資料。
  • 本 App 不會將您的位置長期儲存、不會用於識別您的身分、也不會分享給第三方做廣告或追蹤。代理伺服器僅為提供查詢結果做短暫快取（數分鐘內自動清除）。

2. 本機儲存的資料

  • 收藏的路線、地區選擇、背景顏色、地圖顯示偏好等設定，僅儲存在您的裝置本機，不會上傳。
  • 路線清單等資料會在裝置本機快取以加速載入與減少網路用量。
  • 上述資料會在您解除安裝本 App 時一併刪除。

3. 通知與到站提醒（背景推播）

  • 當您主動開啟「到站提醒」時，本 App 會將下列資料傳送至我們的查詢代理伺服器，用於在公車接近您指定站牌時推播通知：
    • 裝置推播權杖（Push Token）：由 HarmonyOS 推播服務產生，用於將通知送達您的裝置，不含個人身分資訊。
    • 提醒訂閱內容：您所選擇的路線、站牌、所在縣市，以及提醒提前分鐘數。
  • 上述資料僅儲存於我們的代理伺服器（Cloudflare），用途僅限於提供到站提醒服務，不會用於廣告，也不會分享給第三方。
  • 提醒為一次性：通知發送後即自動刪除該筆訂閱；未達提醒條件者最長保留 1 小時後自動清除。您亦可隨時在 App 內取消提醒，取消後對應資料會立即從伺服器刪除。
  • 若您未開啟到站提醒功能，本 App 不會蒐集或傳送上述任何資料。

二、第三方服務

  • 交通部 TDX 運輸資料流通服務：公車路線、到站時間、站牌等資料來源。
  • 查詢代理伺服器（twbus.mshw.info）：本 App 自架的 Cloudflare 服務，用於轉送資料查詢、快取結果以降低流量；當您啟用「到站提醒」時，亦會於此暫存您的推播權杖與提醒訂閱（路線、站牌、縣市、提前分鐘），僅供發送提醒之用，發送後或取消時即刪除。除此之外不蒐集個人身分資訊。
  • 華為推播服務（HarmonyOS Push Kit）：用於在背景將到站提醒送達您的裝置；適用華為之隱私政策。
  • OpenStreetMap：地圖圖資（當您選擇線上地圖顯示時）。
  • 華為地圖 / Petal Maps：當您點選「導航前往」或「周邊探索」時，開啟外部地圖 App，後續行為適用該服務之隱私政策。

三、權限說明

  • 位置：查詢附近站牌。
  • 網路：取得公車即時資料、訂閱與發送到站提醒。
  • 通知：發送到站提醒。

四、兒童隱私

本 App 為一般大眾交通查詢工具，不會刻意蒐集兒童之個人資料。

五、政策變更

本政策若有更新，將於本頁公告並更新「最後更新日期」。

六、聯絡我們

如對本政策有任何疑問，請來信：mizuhara@mshw.info

English

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Thank you for using Taiwan Bus Go (the "App"). We respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data We Collect and Use

1.1 Location

  • When you use the "Nearby Stops" feature, the App requests your device location (approximate or precise) to find bus stops near you.
  • Location is accessed only when you actively use this feature, and is forwarded through our query proxy to Taiwan's MOTC TDX service to retrieve nearby stop data.
  • The App does not store your location long-term, does not use it to identify you, and does not share it with third parties for advertising or tracking. The proxy only caches query results briefly (cleared automatically within minutes).

1.2 Data Stored on Your Device

  • Favorite routes, region selection, background color, and map preferences are stored only locally on your device and are never uploaded.
  • Route lists are cached locally to speed up loading and reduce data usage.
  • All such data is removed when you uninstall the App.

1.3 Notifications & Arrival Reminders (Background Push)

  • When you choose to enable an arrival reminder, the App sends the following data to our query proxy server to notify you when a bus approaches your selected stop:
    • Device Push Token: generated by the HarmonyOS Push service to deliver the notification to your device; it contains no personally identifying information.
    • Reminder subscription details: the route, stop, and city you selected, and how many minutes ahead you want to be reminded.
  • This data is stored only on our proxy server (Cloudflare), used solely to provide the arrival-reminder service, and is not used for advertising or shared with third parties.
  • Each reminder is one-time: the subscription is deleted automatically once the notification is sent, and any unfired reminder is cleared automatically after at most 1 hour. You may cancel a reminder at any time in the App, and the corresponding data is immediately deleted from the server.
  • If you do not enable the arrival reminder feature, none of the above data is collected or transmitted.

2. Third-Party Services

  • MOTC TDX (Transport Data eXchange): source of bus routes, ETAs, and stop data.
  • Query proxy (twbus.mshw.info): our self-hosted Cloudflare service that forwards data queries and caches results to reduce load; when you enable arrival reminders, it also temporarily stores your push token and reminder subscription (route, stop, city, lead minutes) solely to send reminders, deleted after sending or upon cancellation. It collects no other personally identifying information.
  • Huawei Push (HarmonyOS Push Kit): used to deliver arrival reminders to your device in the background; Huawei's privacy policy applies.
  • OpenStreetMap: map tiles (when online map display is selected).
  • Petal Maps: launched as an external app when you tap "Navigate" or "Explore Nearby"; its own privacy policy applies thereafter.

3. Permissions

  • Location: to find nearby bus stops.
  • Network: to fetch real-time bus data and to subscribe to / deliver arrival reminders.
  • Notifications: to send arrival reminders.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is a general-purpose transit tool and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

6. Contact Us
